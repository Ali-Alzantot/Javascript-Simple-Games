Simple magic card game 

Made by abanoub samy salib .

How To Play :
Choose card from 21 cards don’t click on it  and memorize its number and shape in your mind 
Then click next ,
Now cards will be distributed in 3 columns ,choose the one where your card appear
Repeat last step for more two times , then press show card the card you have chosen will appear .

Note : 
I used  already made library that draw cards and shuffling it and i put my logic to the game .
